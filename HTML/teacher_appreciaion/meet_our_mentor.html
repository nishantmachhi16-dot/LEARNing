Meet Our Mentor

A tribute to the person behind the lectures — their journey, passion, and the impact they've left on every student who walked into their classroom.

person Prof. Gaurav K Buddhadev

Prof. Gaurav K Buddhadev, is a highly accomplished professional with over two decades of experience in the field of technology and digital transformation. Currently serving as a Senior Cloud Program Manager at Oracle, he has led numerous large-scale global projects, impacting organizations across regions like EMEA, North America, and APAC.

He completed his Bachelor of Engineering in Electronics from BVM Engineering College between 2000 and 2004, building a strong technical foundation early in his career. Further enhancing his expertise, he pursued his education at the prestigious Indian Institute of Management (IIM) Calcutta during 2009–2010, which shaped his strategic and managerial capabilities.

What truly sets him apart is not just his professional excellence, but his dedication to sharing knowledge and guiding students. With a perfect blend of real-world experience and a passion for teaching, he has always inspired us to think beyond textbooks and understand the practical side of technology.

To us, he is more than just a professor—he is a mentor who has left a lasting impact on our learning journey.
