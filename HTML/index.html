FAQs About Home Interior Design

Our comprehensive service covers end-to-end design, from initial spatial planning and 3D visualization to material selection and final site execution. We manage the entire lifecycle, including modular furniture installation, lighting, civil works, and interior styling, ensuring your vision is brought to life by our professional design and installation teams.

Costs are determined based on your specific layout, chosen materials, and project scope. We provide a detailed, itemized estimate prior to commencement to ensure full budget clarity. This estimate covers professional design fees, high-grade materials, hardware, and installation services, with no hidden charges throughout the project.

Completion timelines vary based on the scale of your project, material availability, and site conditions. During the initial consultation, we provide a structured schedule based on our standard process. We aim for efficient execution and will maintain consistent communication to keep you updated on progress throughout the project.

Our process is built on clarity: we start with a consultation to understand your requirements, followed by space planning and 3D design approvals. Once finalized, we procure materials and begin onsite installation. Throughout this journey, our project leads supervise the work, ensuring adherence to the agreed-upon design specifications.

We prioritize durability by sourcing industry-standard, high-grade materials and premium hardware for all modular systems and furniture. Our installation is carried out by skilled professionals who follow standardized industry practices. Every phase of the project undergoes routine site checks to ensure the work aligns with our established quality benchmarks.

We stand by the quality of our execution and materials. All hardware and branded components used in your home come with their original manufacturer’s warranties as per industry standards. We provide documentation for these components upon project handover, ensuring you have the necessary details for long-term maintenance.

Safety and order are core to our execution process. Our teams follow professional installation protocols to ensure your site is handled with care. We maintain a clean, organized workspace and strictly adhere to standard safety practices, providing you with a seamless and secure interior transformation experience.
